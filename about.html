About Me

Pron: Dan Vee-ba

Dan Wiebe

Since childhood I've been a passionate traveler - seeking adventure, new cultures, excited to meet new people. Photography became a wonderful medium in which to record these experiences and share those fleeting interactions with those at home.

Coming from the street shooting culture I try to take that spontaneity and embrace the impromptu moments in all aspects of my shooting. As I look through the viewfinder, it becomes crucial to consider how people are represented, especially when photographing minority cultures.

I try to take this approach and core values into all aspects of my work, whether it's music promo shots, gigs, or behind the scenes photography. I try to convey a small aspect of somebody's story and their interaction with the environment.
